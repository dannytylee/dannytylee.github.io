USDA Education Attainment - Web scraping, Transformation, Visualization (Aug 2023)

This was a project for the Economics Research Lab I'm a part of. Goal - scrape data from the U.S. Department of Agriculture collected on the highest level of educational attainment by county and state over time.

- first experience with web-scraping, used Selenium because table not in HTML source
- used os module and pandas to transform data into usable form separated by completed college, completed some college, completed high school only, not completed high school
- tableau part was on my own initiative, besides the one below I also created a dashboard comparing heatmaps between the four categories over time
- In the future I want to add some KPIs (% change from 1970, % change from 2017, rank within state during selected year, county percentile in US during selected year) or forecasts for the line chart
